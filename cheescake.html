Cheesecake Recipe

Description

Cheesecake has always been one of my favourite treats to bake! Such a yummy taste that doesn't require much effort to create.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Water
  • Whipping Cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Plain biscuits

    • Steps

    1. Break biscuits and place in a food processor
    2. Blend until a crumb texture forms
    3. Add butter, salt and sugar (cinammon if you would like as well)
    4. Press together into a pan.
    5. Whip cream, then beat cream cheese and sugar in a seperate bowl. Add vanilla, lemon and salt.
    6. Fold in whipped cream into this mixture
    7. Pour into crust and refrigerate for 6 hours.
