Vanilla Slices Recipe

Description

Although Caramel slices may be another popular baked delight samiliar to this, I personally think that Vanilla slices are definitely worth the try!

Ingredients

  • Butter puff pastry
  • Caster Sugar
  • Egg yolks
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Bean paste
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Icing Sugar

Steps

  1. Add the frozen pastry onto baking paper, and allow to thaw
  2. Place another baking sheet on top and bake until golden.
  3. Add sugar and cornstarch to a bowl and whisk. Then add in the egg yolks and repeat.
  4. Add a 1/2 cup of ho tmilk and whisk. Then add the remainder and vanilla bean paste - keep whisking
  5. Pour the mixture into a saucepan and stir until it thickens.Then take it off the heat and add the butter
  6. Run your now made custard through a sieve and into the pastry, before letting it cool for 2 hours.
